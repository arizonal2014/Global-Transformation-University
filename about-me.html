GLOBAL TRANSFORMATION UNIVERSITY

About Us

The journey of global transformation remarkably began in 19th century industrialisation, cascading as it did into 20th century wonders and revolutions that captured the magnificence of innovation and creativity on which 21st century disruptions find their sophisticated expressions. These innovative expressions are now pushing the boundaries of human potential across board. This is the legacy on which the Global Transformation University draws inspiration as we create the future we want, a future where the aspirations of people can be fulfilled.

We are a 21st century dream that rests on the transnationalization of education, premised on pragmatic ideals of accessibility and applicability. Simply put, we are a vision of hopes for a better tomorrow through the transformation of minds. For to learn is to grow and to grow is to live. Our life long learning offer gives you a thousand chances to journey through a learning experience that believes in the power of your agency to change the world by changing your world. Therefore, GTU courses are curated with quality and excellence, rooted in core principles of a global classroom model that honors diversity, community, integrity, accessibility, affordability and applicable to problems today and tomorrow.

Our GLOBAL CAMPUS is ready to provide you with a system of advantages through:

1. Open Learning which is suitable for for university students, recent graduates, the unemployed, high School Leavers, informal sector and those with a desire to learn transformative skills that can enhance their lives.

2. Our Professional Development package is the hallmark of todays career development and progression training that we recommend for Young Professionals, Entrepreneurs, graduates and anyone who wants to advance further in their career with practical skills and competencies that can strategically position them for new opportunities.

3. Our Executive Education package gives you the best of both worlds and is more specifically tailored for tailored for executives, managers, directors, parastatals, leaders and other specialised career paths. We are a 21st century hub of knowledge, built with the full understanding that the unlearned among us and beyond will not be those who cant read or write but those who cant learn, unlearn and relearn.

Our Global Campus is open to all learners at all levels through our carefully curated packages that leave no one behind. Choose the best package for your personal development, organisational needs and national transformation strategic goals.
